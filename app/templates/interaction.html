Interactive RTA Prediction Map

This interactive map allows you to find accident hotspots along an optimized driving route between two locations in Greater London at a particular time.

To use the map, please pick :
(1) a date/time in the next 48 hours,
(2) your origin and
(3) your destination

NOTE:- Refresh page if there are no dropdown boxes in the map. Please note that accident points may take a while to load.
